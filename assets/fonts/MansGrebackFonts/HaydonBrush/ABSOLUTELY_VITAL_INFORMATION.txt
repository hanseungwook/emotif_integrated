Haydon Brush is a handwritten brush typeface.
It is staunch and confident, the perfect type to use for a diagonal slogan or a logotype. 

The font contains ligatures, smaller case contextual and stylitic alternates, as well as a full upper case alternate alphabet. 

Designed by Måns Grebäck, it is guaranteed to give your projects the extra character it needs.
